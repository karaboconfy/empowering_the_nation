SME was developed to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. SME is also a training school of making gardeners and domestic workers more marketable when seeking employment and enable people to have their employees upskill to offer more skilled services to household. Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg when hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programs to empower themselves and provide more marketable skills
